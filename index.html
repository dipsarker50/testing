About the Project

Welcome to the Smart Dustbin Management system! This innovative platform uses IoT technology to monitor dustbins in real-time. It helps prioritize which dustbins need to be cleaned first based on their fullness, gas levels, and environmental conditions.

Features

  • Real-time monitoring of dustbin levels.
  • Automatic prioritization for cleaning.
  • Analysis of gas, temperature, and humidity.
